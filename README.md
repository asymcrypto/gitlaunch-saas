## 📌 What is GitLaunch?

**GitLaunch** is an **AI-powered, open-source SaaS launchpad** that helps developers and teams **build, ship, and scale** SaaS products — directly from a **GitHub repository**.

> Describe the SaaS you want → GitLaunch generates the code, features, docs, modules, launch plan & monetization setup.

Whether you're a **solo indie hacker**, a **team**, or an **open-source community**, GitLaunch gives you the **full toolkit to turn ideas into revenue-ready SaaS products — fast.**
